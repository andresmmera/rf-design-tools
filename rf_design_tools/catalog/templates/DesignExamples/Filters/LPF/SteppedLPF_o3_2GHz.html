Microstrip Stepped LPF

A microstrip stepped impedance lowpass filter is designed on a 20 mil thickness RO4003C substrate in order to meet the following design goals:

Parameter Value Band
Insertion loss < 1 dB [0, 2] GHz
Stopband rejection > 10 dB > 4 GHz
Return loss < -15 dB [0, 2] GHz

The first step is to determine the order of the filter that satifies the design goal. For doing this, the filter design tool included in this website is used.

Ideal Stepped LPF Circuit
Theoretical Frequency Response

Then, the transmission lines are synthesized using AWR TX-Line tool (free). Notice that the high impedance lines must be wide enough to be fabricated into a standard PCB manufacturing facility, so let's keep them wider than 100 μm. Once the physical dimensions of the filter are calculated, the design is laid out and assembleD into Sonnet Lite (it's free, too!)
